At the kayarmstead center of communication disorders, we provide a full-range of services for people with various communicative and acquired communication disorders from birth through adulthood. our mission is to be an equal partner in the community by providing high-quality, evidenced-based services that meet the needs of all people. We also have a speech language and hearing assessment program for children and adults as well as a school-based early childhood intervention program. Our goal is to improve the lives of individuals who have been affected by their own communication difficulties.
