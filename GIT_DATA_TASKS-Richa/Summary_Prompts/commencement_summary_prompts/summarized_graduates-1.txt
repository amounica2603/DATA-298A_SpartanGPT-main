San Jose State University will hold commencement ceremonies for all students in the spring and summer of 2023. Each student will receive a complimentary guest ticket to attend the ceremony. If a student removes from the ceremony because of any reason, they will receive six free guest tickets. Guest tickets are not necessary for participating graduates; they are only available for the ceremony listed on their guest ticket. guests age three-three and up are required to purchase academic regalia. orders can be made at the partan bookstore on campus. medallions may be purchased from vendors but must match degree levels/degrees. graduation gifts are also available for pre order and on site purchasing. The university follows the California Department of Public Health's "mega indoor events" policy. everyone is asked to use the sick check tool before entering the university.
