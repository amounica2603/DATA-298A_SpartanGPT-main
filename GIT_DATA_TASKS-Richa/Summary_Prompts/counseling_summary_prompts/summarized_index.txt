San Jose State University offers a wide range of mental health and psychological services to assist students, families, and the community. If you need immediate assistance, please call us at 408- 924-5910. If you have an urgent situation, please visit our student wellness center in room 300b or call the university's police office at 427-804-2222. After-hours crises can be solved by calling the local suicide prevention line or by sending a message for help.
