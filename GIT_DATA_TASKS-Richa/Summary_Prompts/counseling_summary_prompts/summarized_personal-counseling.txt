The counselors at the university offer brief counseling services to students and couples who wish to seek help but are not in significant distress. individual counseling helps people to resolve their personal problems and challenges according to the specific needs of the person. sometimes people who care for each other encounter issues in their relationships and it is important that they be able to discuss their own problems in a constructive manner. counselors keep confidential information about you in your counseling records. If you are prescribed medicine by a doctor at caps, this information is kept separate from your academic or health records.
