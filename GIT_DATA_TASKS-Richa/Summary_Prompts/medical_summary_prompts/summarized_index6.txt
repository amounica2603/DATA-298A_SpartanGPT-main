The student health center charges a small fee to cover the basic services students receive at the university.i.e., general check-ups, lab work, and sexual exams are all free.reproductive care is available for low-income students at no cost.gynetic therapy is available at no expense.psychological treatment is not required for students who have received a diagnosis of tuberculosis or another form of birth control.all other services are offered at a greatly reduced cost.preventative care is also offered for students with a pre-existing condition.student pharmacists are also on staff to perform laboratory tests and over-the counter drugs.
