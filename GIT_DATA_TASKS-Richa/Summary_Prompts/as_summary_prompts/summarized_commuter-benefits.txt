The pre-tax commuter benefit program is a tax-free program designed to help eligible employees save money by deferring the cost of purchasing transit passes. If you sign up for it, you'll be able to defer your monthly transportation bill and pay less than you would have been if you didn't sign up. Once you're enrolled in the program, you can take advantage of all the benefits that the commuter check program has to offer.you can use your credit card to buy transit passes or tickets, or cash value at any of the participating transit centers.
