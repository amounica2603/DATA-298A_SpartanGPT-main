The student organization's mandatory funding training will be held on March 10, 2023, March 3, 2023 and April 14, 2023. If you have questions about how to get started or if you need any additional information, please contact the office of Student Involvement at 408) 926-4240. A copy of the budget policy is also available for anyone interested in starting a club or an organization. To submit a request for funds, students must complete a form four weeks before the date of their program, event, or activity. Each student organization can receive up to two-thirds of the total amount requested. groups may request more than that amount but must submit all required documents within four weeks.
