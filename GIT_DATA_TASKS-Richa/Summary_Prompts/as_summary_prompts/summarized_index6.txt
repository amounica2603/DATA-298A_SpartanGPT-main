San Jose State University has adopted a "dismount zone" for electric, motorized, and skateboards that can be used to go to campus but must be left in the designated drop off zones. These areas are located on the perimeter of the university and are enforced by fire code regulations. persons who violate this policy may be charged with citations under California vehicle code 21225 or 21235 and other applicable college regulations. these regulations do not impact electricmobility for anay purposes.
