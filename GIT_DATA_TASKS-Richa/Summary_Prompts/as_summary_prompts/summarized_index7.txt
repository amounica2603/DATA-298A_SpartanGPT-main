San Jose State University has two on campus and two off campus garages. An additional lot is available for those who wish to use it as a ride lot. These lots are not free, but they do have to be purchased. The university also has a shuttle service between the South Campus Parking Lot and Duncan Hall. paid parking is required all times when there is a car park. electric vehicle drivers must use the charging stations in the north and south parking garages or lot 4, respectively.
