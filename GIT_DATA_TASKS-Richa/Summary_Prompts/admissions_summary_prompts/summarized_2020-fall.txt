All majors at San Jose State University compete for seats in the major based on an applicant's college eligibility index. For fall 2020, all majors are impacted and all freshmen compete for space in their major. Based on the applicants' college education institution's eligibility index, all students compete for spots in the minor.freshmen who have not yet graduated from high school will be given an increased preference in the state university's "eligibility index," which is calculated by multiplying the student's score on the sass exam or the act exam.if you have met this threshold, you may be admitted to your alternate major.
