All majors at SJsu are affected for transfers. All transfer applicants compete for seats in the major based on their cumulative Gpa. All domestic transfer applicants need to submit a separate application for fall admission. For fall 2019, all applicants must have completed at least two course requirements. If they do not complete those requirements, they will be admitted as unaccompanies.
	San Jose State University is conveniently located in the city of San Jose, just outside of town.
