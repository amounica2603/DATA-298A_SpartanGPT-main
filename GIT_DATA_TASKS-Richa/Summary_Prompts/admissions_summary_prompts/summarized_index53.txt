All majors at SJU are impacted by freshmen. Freshmen compete for space in each major based on their eligible-eligibility index at the start of application.freshmen who meet the university's minimum requirements will be offered an increased preference, and local applicants will be given a slightly higher preference.In addition, additional consideration is given to applicants that qualify for the Cal State apply application fee Waiver; are first-generation college students; or are a U.S. veteran.
