Sjsu does not consider any changes made to your Calstate apply application during the Prior Filing Period, December a. All majors are impacted by transferable courses. For fall 2020, applicants must have completed at least a combined amount of transferable course requirements. Majors that do not have sufficient numbers of enrolled students to meet the capacity of their respective majors will be admitted as unaccompanies.
	Undergraduate applicants must have completed at least two courses in order to be considered for admission. If they do not complete all of their course requirements, they are admitted above the minimum acceptable GPA. This major does not have the ability to admit applicants without completing any course requirements.
