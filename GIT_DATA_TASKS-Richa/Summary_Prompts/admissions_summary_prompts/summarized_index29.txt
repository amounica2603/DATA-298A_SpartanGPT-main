Eop is a student assistance program that provides financial assistance, housing, and other services to eligible sex students during their bachelor's or master's studies at San Jose State University. Students who have been unable to attend school because of financial hardship are eligible to apply for eops. the guardian scholar program provides support services for unaccompanied youth, homeless youth, young adults, and youth in legal custody. the partan east side promises a partnership between sanjos state university and partan high school district.
