All majors at SJSU are impacted by freshmen. Freshmen who meet the university's minimum eligibility requirements will be offered space in their major.freshmen who do not meet the required scores will be ranked on an "eligibility index" that takes into account both the student's performance in class and his or her overall college readiness.all majors have an eligible applicant's score, which determines whether or not they will be admitted to the major. For fall 20, local applicants receive a preference of a 0.5 g pa increase in the calculations for those who will graduated from a school in San Jose Clara County.in addition, additional consideration is given to applicants whom qualify for the Cal State apply application fee-waiver.
