If you miss an admissions deadline, you will be unable to attend the university for the term that you applied.freshman and domestic applicants are both required to submit an application for admission directly after high school.if you miss one of these deadlines, you can withdraw your application and become a part-time student at San Josee State University.you must meet all of the steps in the process to be considered for admission. freshman is an international student who plans to attend on a "f1 or j1 visa" and applies directly after graduating from high school; this means that they do not have to wait until the end of their senior year to be admitted. Freshman is also an applicant who intends to attend SJsu on an F1 or J1 visa.he/she does not need to complete any additional preparatory exercises during the apply process.
