The majors at Sjsu compete for seats in the major based on student's cumulative GPA. All transfer applicants who applied during the Prior Application period must have submited a separate application to be considered for Fall 2022 admission, which will take place after all transfer applicants have completed their required courses. In addition, all local applicants are offered an increase in their grade point average as a result of receiving the majority of those transferable units from a Santa Clara or St. C.C. college.
