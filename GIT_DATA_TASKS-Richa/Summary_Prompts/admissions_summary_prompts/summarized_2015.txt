All majors at SJU are impacted by freshmen. applicants compete for seats in the major based on their Csu Eligibility Initiation Score.all majors attend SJ U.C. during the fall and spring of each year, there are sufficient numbers of freshmen to ensure that all majors meet both individual capacity and overall University Enrollment Capacity.freshmen who have not yet graduated from a school in San Jose Clara County receive preference in the university's admission process.appearing to an undergraduate or visiting campus is the best way to get started.if you have any questions about applying to graduate or undergraduate programs, please visit our graduate admissions office.
