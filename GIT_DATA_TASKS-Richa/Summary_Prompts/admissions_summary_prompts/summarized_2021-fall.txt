All majors at SJsu are affected for freshmen. Freshmen who meet the university's minimum eligibility requirements will be ranked on an "eligibility index" that determines whether they will be offered admission to each major.freshmen who have met the required amount of college preparatory schooling will be invited to all majors, regardless of their rank in the major.in addition, local applicants who have graduated from high school in San Jose will be given an increased preference as a result of a slight increase in the calculations for those who will graduate after completing a bachelor's degree from a nearby high school.specifically, applicants who qualified for the Cal State apply application fee-waiver will be considered first-generation and/or veterans.
