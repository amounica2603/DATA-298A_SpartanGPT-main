All majors at SJU are impacted by freshmen. For fall, all applicants compete for space in one of the majors. Majors with limited or no other availability in the college compete for seats in the major.freshmen who have completed their high school in San Jose and will not be admitted to the university receive preference in the application process.majors that do not meet the university's minimum requirements may be admitted at an above-the-csu standard.non-regular residents must have met higher standards.if you are interested in applying to undergraduate or graduate programs, please visit our undergraduate students and recruitment office.
