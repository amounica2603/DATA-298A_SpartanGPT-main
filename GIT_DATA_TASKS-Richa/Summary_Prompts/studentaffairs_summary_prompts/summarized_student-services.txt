San jose State University's student affairs office helps students learn about themselves and build on their strengths, while offering a variety of services to support their education and career goals. student counselors help students prepare for the next steps in their life after college. case managers help students find the resources they need to recover from their experiences at SJSU.student health center promotes health and well being, provide supports for emotionally or relationship concerns and offer medical, prescriptions and immuniizations.vester's resource center provides financial aid and legal assistance for un documented students
