San jose State University provides deaf and hard of hearing services for academic support only. Eligibility for these services is determined on an individual basis each semester, and is provided until the end of instruction.unauthorized recording, dissemination or publication of handwritten notes for selected course/s are subject to discipline; and may lead to suspension of notetaker services.the accompanies students in their studies by providing them with equal access to the curriculum.the accommodations of extended assignment are prescribed to support limited abilities due to adversity.the management of such limitations is essential to student success.the provision of extended assignments consist of three steps: Students must request accommodations before the need arises; students must discuss the accommodation with each instructor; and students must meet with one another to discuss the accommodations.
