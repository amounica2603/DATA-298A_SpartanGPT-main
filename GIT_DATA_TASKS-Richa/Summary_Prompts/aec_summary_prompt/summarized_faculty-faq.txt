Aec accommodations process information. Students must request their accommodation via myAec, in order to get a faculty notice letter from the instructor. students will then send an email to the instructor at least five days before the exam date. If the instructor does not receive a note from the student that the student cannot take on a particular date and time, the instructor must inform the student of the student's accommodations. Students should discuss their prescribed test accommodations with each professor of record. instructors can use canvas tutorials to help students set up exams with longer time. Notetaking is provided to students who are unable to take notes during class because of a verifiable physical or mental disability. These accommodations include notetaking services, attendance accommodations, and extended assignment accommodations.
	students will learn how to submit requests for print copies of books in various different formats. These requests are handled in a manner that does not compromise the quality of the product.
