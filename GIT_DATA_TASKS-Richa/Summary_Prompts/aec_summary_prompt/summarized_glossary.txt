Do It, Glossary of Disability-Relational terms: technology that helps people with a physical or mental impairment. A voice recognition program allows students to use voice commands to navigate the school's various learning tools. Students sign an e-Agreement every semester for accommodations on Myaec, and faculty receives a notice letter outlining their accommodations. Students take notetaker services for students who have a disability and are not allowed to take notes during course lectures. The student portal allows them to view their accommodations and schedule exams, and the faculty portal gives professors access to manage all students and all classes in one location.
