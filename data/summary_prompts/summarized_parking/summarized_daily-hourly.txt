Students, employees, vendors, visitors may purchase and pay for their daily and hourly parking permits through park-mobile app or self-serve permit pay stations. the general guidelines below apply to all forms of physical permits. customers must enter their vehicle information correctly in order to avoid having to pay a fee. parks-app and pay-stations partner to offer a service whereby people can pay for hours of space at a fixed price. there is no annual fee to purchase a single day or hourly permit; however, there is a monthly fee that is charged when individuals begin a session.
