The student advisory committee has been an important part of the college since 1986. it advises the dean on any student problem or need, recommends policy or suggestions to the college, and suggests improvements. the students who participate in the committee are the representatives between the department and school, the students at large, and the Dean's Office. the committee aims to advance the Mission of the College of Health and Human Sciences as a Comprehensive College committed to preparation students to be engagement and productive leaders
