Carhs: A Resource for Chhs Faculty is a free resource center for faculty. Editing and statistics consulting services are also available for free at the carhs. This workshop will provide tips and resources on authorship, acknowledgements, and manuscript development. it will also serve as a good opportunity for students to discuss their own work with other scholars. If you are not able to attend, but would like to hear a recording from the session, please register. After submitting, you will get a confirmation of your attendance. Other special events are planned in the coming week.
	The next day of the annual meeting will be held at the university's health science center. If you're an early career or tenure track faculty member, come to the meeting and learn more about the cher institute, which is a national institute for general medical sciences that's been funded by a federally-administered "noble" grant.
