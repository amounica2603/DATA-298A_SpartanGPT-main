Students who need an alternative experience must submit a request to the dean. If approved, students will be paired with an international student and engaged in peer-to-peer interaction while enrolled at chas 198 itl. students who choose volunteer service are required to complete 10 hours of volunteer work in a community service organization where they live from a different culture than their own. This option is not limited to students who have significant international experience within five years of entering the university. students may petition for a change of the requirements. examples include, but are not limited: international service as a member of the peace corps, schooling, or military service abroad; work experience abroad; or independent experience outside of the university's sponsored programs.
