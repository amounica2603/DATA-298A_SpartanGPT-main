Dr. Shillington has been appointed dean of the college of human sciences at San jose state university, and is also a professor of epidemiology at the coloredado School of Public Health. She comes to San jose as dean because she has served as director for 15 years and helped create the "nih-funded center for alcohol/drug studies and services." She has been a member of the board of directors for the state's alcohol and drug study and services program and has been involved in several grants totaling more than $14 million.
