Lucias College and Graduate School of Business is the largest source of business graduates in the valley. The college's mission is to prepare students for a variety of career options, from entry-level engineering to entrepreneurship. Charles Wilks Davidson College of Engineering is the #1 engineering college in the world. The colleges also serve as advocates for the San Jose State graduate student population. College of graduate studies creates a national center for advocated health and human science education; the college serves as an advocate for the state's 6,000 graduate students. College Of Humanities and the Arts provides a liberal arts education that emphasizes creativity, innovation, problem-solving, and critical thinkers. The College of Professional and Global Education provides students with exciting new ways to get to college. Serving adults as well as international and local students, the college offers graduate degrees and certificate, professional development, and corporate training.
