The critical bilingual authorization path is a pathway for teachers to obtain teacher licensure and become certified as teachers in dual-immersion or bilingual classrooms in claudio guaiado's school district. educalists are also invited to hear more about the program from two of the college's professors: david zapaz and edraardo mujioz. They discuss how they have been able to use their grants toward professional development during the pandemic, and how they've prepared them to be transformative teachers.
