If you're looking for a job as a school counselor in California, you can get your degree from a nearby college or university and apply directly to the San Jose State University's School of Social Work's Pupil Personnel Services credential program. Students who haven't earned a bachelor's degree will need to apply through the Ma in counseling and Guidance program option. This program prepares students for work as school counsellors in public schools in California. According to the state's new standards for school counselors, "companionship and guidance" is needed to promote student learning and advancement.
