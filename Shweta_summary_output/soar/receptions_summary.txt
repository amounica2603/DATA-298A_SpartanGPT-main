This is the fifth annual bridge the bay event and it will be held on Saturday, March 11th from 9:00 am to 11:30 am at the SJsu University in downtown Oakland. A special team of Sjsu students and professional staff will be there to greet you and answer any questions you may have about the process to enroll at SJ U. A light breakfast will be provided as well as information about how to find out more about the university and its resources.
