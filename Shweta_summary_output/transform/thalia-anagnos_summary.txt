Anagnos's career at San Josee State University is described as fulfilling. She has been a part of the university for 30 years and has been involved in many committees and other roles. As an assistant professor, she was promoted to the chair of the department of civil and environment engineering. At the same time, she became the university's director of assessment. Her role at the university also allowed her to develop her expertise in earthquake engineering. In addition to her work with the university, she works on a new edition of an engineering textbook written by three female faculty members.
	Anagnos explains how she came to be a student at the university and how she has been an inspiration to other students throughout her life. Her mother was a great teacher and community organizer who always encouraged her to do what she chose in whatever endeavor she chose. She says that every day is different and that every year is different.
