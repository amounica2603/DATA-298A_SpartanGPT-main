In the winter of 2019, Gulliver graduated from the Lucias School of Business in the spring of 2019. He's now working as the Events Manager for the National Alliance on mental illness and he performs standup comedy Under the moniker, "kyle From Work". After graduating from the school, he joins many other extracurricular activities to help him find his own creative outlet. He works for an advertising company named "butter" and co-founds a local arts organization called 10fam Collective. Together, they host shows across the South Bay to share their love of music and poetry. They also hold fund raisers for philanthropy and aapp mental health. At the end of the year, if you're wondering where he's going with this story, don't worry because he has a job lined up for him at the Native American Mental Health Collaborative.
