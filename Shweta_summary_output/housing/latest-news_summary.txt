If you're looking for a place to live at Sjsu, please fill out the housing application and submit it before the end of the academic year. If you haven't yet considered living at the International House, there's still time to apply. The International House is open to both U.S. students and international students, so anyone can apply! any student who wants to cancel their housing contract with university services will be given a credit on the initial payment as well as any additional charges paid to University housing services.
