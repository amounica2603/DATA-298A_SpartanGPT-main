The center of campus village is the 15 story tower with three, four, five bedroom single apartments and three bedroom double apartments all equipped with kitchens and living room areas. single apartment building b has one shared living space, a large kitchen with an oven and fullsize refrigerator and a few bathrooms depending on how many occupants there are in the building. double apartment buildings have only one set of extra long beds that can either be separated or been bunked. junior apartment buildings include the same services as the double rooms but at a slightly lower rate.
