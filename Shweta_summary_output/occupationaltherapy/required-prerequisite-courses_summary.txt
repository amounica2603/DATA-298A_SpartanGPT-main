At least four of the eight required courses must be completed in order to enroll in the occupational therapy program. if an individual takes an anatomy course not having a laboratory as part of the course, that course must be taken over a period of two semesters with a lab at the end of each quarter. Sjsu also offers an e-learning course that fulfills this requirement each summer. This course is offered through open university and does not need to be completed prior to taking it.
